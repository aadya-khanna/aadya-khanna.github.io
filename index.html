Party In the Park

Saturday April 30th


Come and join us at ISP for the Party in the Park: fun, food and live music. Bring a blanket or lawn chair kick back, relax and be entertained while the children are involved in good active fun. Admission free with paid activities, proceeds going to local charities supporting ukrainian refugees. Hope to see you here.
